The Diagnosis
Prescription without a diagnosis — what we are actually solving for
⚠The current framework solves the symptom of tool sprawl. The real problem is the absence of an operating model capable of driving customer adoption and consumption of agentic enterprise solutions at speed, at scale, and with specificity for every customer.
Gap 01
Strategy without infrastructure
We have a vision for agent-first innovation but no operating model to execute it. Tools are adopted ad hoc. There is no mechanism to turn field learning into organizational capability.
Gap 02
Adoption without outcomes
Engineers measure adoption as token consumption. ProServ and Solutions measure it as the outcome a solution delivers. Without a unified definition, we cannot connect AI activity to business results.
Gap 03
Innovation without compounding
Similar solutions are built in parallel with no sharing mechanism. Field learning disappears at the end of each engagement. The org is not getting smarter with every customer interaction.
What the symptoms look like
Too many tools — no front door, no qualification
High token consumption, low measurable output
Context lost at every handoff: SE → FDE → ProServ → CS
No unified definition of "adoption" across orgs
What the framework actually solves
One intake loop — signal to governance, end-to-end
Output-based enablement — fluency, not completion
Structured handoffs — every transition is a data event
Unified definitions — one metric language across all orgs
